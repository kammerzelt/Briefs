Contrast therapy pool New in 2026

Pool solar heater Accelerating
Energy cost anxiety is driving solar heating research that wasn't present at this volume before 2025. The tariff environment and rising utility rates are accelerating the trend. Peaks in spring and fall — shoulder season heating is the use case.

Natural swimming pool Mainstream crossover
After years as a niche European concept, natural pool searches hit a US volume threshold worth tracking in late 2025. Shelter media coverage (HGTV, AD) is driving consumer awareness. Builder inquiry will follow 12–18 months behind the search curve.
